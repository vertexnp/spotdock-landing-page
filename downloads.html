Get SpotDock

Ready to elevate your music experience? Follow the steps below to get started.

Download for macOS
SpotDock 1.0.0 • macOS 12.0 or later
1

Download

Click the button above to download the SpotDock .dmg file to your Mac.

2

Install

Open the disk image and drag SpotDock to your Applications folder.

3

Launch

Open SpotDock from your Applications folder or via Spotlight Search.

"App is damaged" or "Unidentified Developer"

Since SpotDock is not yet code-signed by Apple, you may see a warning. This is normal for independent software.

The Fix: Right-click (or Control-click) the SpotDock app in your Applications folder and select Open. In the dialog that appears, click Open again. You only need to do this once.

Privacy Policy & Permissions

To control your music, SpotDock requires Automation and Accessibility permissions.

1. Go to System Settings > Privacy & Security.
2. Under Automation, ensure SpotDock is allowed to control Spotify.
3. Under Accessibility, ensure SpotDock is toggled ON.

Still having issues?

Make sure you are using the official Spotify Desktop App. SpotDock cannot control the Spotify web player or mobile versions.
